Home Appliance Checklist

Must-have features for a modern home.

Kitchen Essentials

  • Refrigerator: Choose energy-efficient models with smart cooling technology.
  • Oven & Stove: Gas or electric? Consider your cooking style and efficiency needs.
  • Dishwasher: Look for quiet operation and water-saving features.

Laundry Room Must-Haves

  • Washing Machine: Front-load or top-load? High-efficiency models save water and energy.
  • Dryer: Consider sensor drying technology for efficiency.
  • Iron & Steamer: Keep your clothes looking fresh and wrinkle-free.

Outdoor & Grilling Essentials

  • Grill: Choose between gas, charcoal, or electric grills for outdoor cooking.
  • Outdoor Refrigerator: Keep drinks and food cool for barbecues.
  • Patio Heater: Extend outdoor gatherings during cooler months.

Final Thoughts

Ensuring you have the right appliances makes everyday tasks more convenient. Use this checklist as a guide for outfitting your home with efficiency and comfort in mind.
